About me|

Glad to see you there!

My name is Maria Artamonova, I am a Junior front-end developer. I create interactive, responsive sites, that work on most popular browsers. To reach this result, I use modern tools, such as: HTML5, CSS3, SCSS, BEM, Flexbox, Bootstrap, Git, JavaScript (ES6+), DOM, Webpack, Babel.

To do my work better, I regularly attend front-end conferences in Yandex, Rambler&Co, Mail.ru, meetups, follow the news of the world of web-development and can not image my life without regular studying. In 2017 I have finished course of "Basics of HTML, CSS, JS" in Moscow School of Coding. Worked in a web-studio as an HTML-coder.

With me you will not have problems with the timely creation of your site, because in 2016 I graduated with honors from Ulyanovsk State University in the direction of "management of organization" and constantly develop myself in the field of management.

I have skills in digital design, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator at the level of creating banners, so I can make you a website according to your layout and, if it would be necessary, can help you with its design.

Let's have a good cooperation!
